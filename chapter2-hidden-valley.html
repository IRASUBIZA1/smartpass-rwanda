Chapter 2: The Secret Behind The Valley

After leaving the library, Ethan and Maya walked home together. Neither of them spoke much because their minds were filled with questions.

The old map was unlike anything they had ever seen. It showed mountains, rivers, and symbols that neither of them understood.

"Do you really believe this valley exists?" Maya asked.

Ethan looked at the map carefully.

"I don't know," he replied. "But someone created this map for a reason."

That evening, Ethan studied the map under the light of a small lamp. He noticed a strange symbol drawn near the center.

It looked like a tree with deep roots spreading across the ground.

The next day, Ethan and Maya searched the school library for information about the symbol.

After hours of searching, they found an old history book hidden on the highest shelf.

Inside the book was a picture of the same symbol.

Below it were words written by someone many years ago:

"The Valley of Amani protects the knowledge of those who seek wisdom, not power."

Maya looked surprised.

"So this place was real..."

Ethan nodded.

"But why did people hide it?"

They continued reading and discovered that the valley was once visited by great thinkers, inventors, and teachers.

However, some people wanted to use the knowledge inside the valley only for themselves.

To protect it, the guardians of the valley disappeared and kept its location secret.

"The valley is not hidden because it is lost," Maya said.

"It is hidden because it is waiting for the right people to find it."

Ethan looked at the map again.

For the first time, he wondered if the map had somehow found him.

That night, Ethan made a decision.

He and Maya would follow the map and discover the truth behind the Hidden Valley of Amani.

But they did not know that someone else had already discovered their secret.

From a distance, a mysterious figure watched the two students.

"The map has returned," the stranger whispered.

"And this time, I will find the valley first."
