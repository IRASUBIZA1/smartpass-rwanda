Chapter 1: The First Meeting Under The Mango Tree

In a small village surrounded by green hills, there stood an old mango tree. For generations, the tree had been a place where people rested, shared stories, and dreamed about the future.

Amina often visited the tree after school. She loved sitting under its wide branches while reading books and planning her dream of becoming a doctor.

"One day, I will help people who have no one to help them," she whispered.

One afternoon, while writing notes under the tree, she heard someone nearby.

It was David, a young student carrying a bag full of books.

"I'm sorry," David said. "I did not know anyone was here."

Amina smiled.

"It's okay. This tree is big enough for two people."

David sat down and they started talking.

They discovered that they both loved learning and wanted to make a difference in their community.

"I want to build things that improve people's lives," David said.

"And I want to heal people," Amina replied.

For the first time, they realized they had found someone who understood their dreams.

Days passed, and they continued meeting under the mango tree. They talked about school, their families, and their hopes for the future.

Neither of them knew that their friendship would soon face a great challenge.

Because hidden behind their smiles was a history between their families. A history that could change everything.

The mango tree had witnessed many stories before...

But this was the beginning of the most unforgettable one.
