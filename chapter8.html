📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 8: A Lesson Beyond Wealth

Adrian walked out of the house with anger written across his face.

He could not understand why Linka was still standing outside his gate after being told to leave.

As he approached, he began speaking harshly.

"Why are you still here?" he demanded. "Didn't the guards tell you to go?"

Linka tried to answer, but her voice was weak.

She had barely eaten for days.

The stress, the fear, and the constant struggle had drained her strength.

Suddenly, her legs gave way.

She collapsed onto the ground.

At first, Adrian thought she was pretending.

He stood there, waiting for her to get up.

But she did not move.

A small wound on her arm began to bleed where she had fallen.

Minutes passed.

The guards looked nervous.

Something was clearly wrong.

For the first time, Adrian felt fear.

"Call the driver!" he shouted.

Together they rushed Linka to the nearest clinic.

The journey felt endless.

As doctors worked to save her, Adrian sat outside the treatment room.

His hands trembled.

He kept replaying everything that had happened since the day they met.

After what felt like hours, a doctor finally came out.

"Doctor, is she alright?" Adrian asked immediately.

The doctor looked at him seriously.

"She was very close to dying."

Adrian's face turned pale.

"But I didn't do anything," he replied.

The doctor sighed.

"This is not about violence. She is severely weak from hunger and exhaustion."

Those words struck Adrian deeply.

For the first time, he realized that while he had been living a comfortable life, someone his own age had been fighting a battle just to survive.

When Linka finally opened her eyes, she found Adrian sitting beside her bed.

His expression was different now.

There was no anger.

Only regret.

"I'm sorry," Adrian said quietly.

And for the first time, Linka saw genuine kindness in his eyes.
