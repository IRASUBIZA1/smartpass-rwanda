📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 12: A Door Opens

The days following the loss of her mother's belongings were difficult for Linka.

Although she tried to remain strong, the pain stayed in her heart.

Adrian noticed that she smiled less and spent more time alone.

One afternoon, while visiting the village market, Adrian received a phone call from one of his friends in the city.

His friend worked for a large modeling and creative talent agency.

As they talked, Adrian suddenly remembered Linka's designs.

An idea immediately came to his mind.

Over the next few days, he gathered photos of Linka's work and sent them to the agency.

He did not tell Linka what he was doing.

He wanted to be certain before giving her hope.

A week later, the response arrived.

The agency was impressed.

They wanted to meet Linka.

When Adrian received the message, he could hardly contain his excitement.

That evening, he found Linka sitting beneath her favorite tree.

"I have something important to tell you," he said.

Linka looked up with curiosity.

Adrian explained everything.

At first, she thought he was joking.

"Someone in the city wants to see my work?" she asked.

Adrian nodded.

"They believe you have talent, Linka. Real talent."

For several seconds, she could not speak.

All her life, people had focused on her problems.

Very few had noticed her abilities.

Now, for the first time, a door was opening.

A door that could lead to a completely different future.

But accepting the opportunity would mean leaving the village behind.

Leaving the only home she had ever known.

As the sun slowly disappeared beyond the hills, Linka realized that the biggest decision of her life was standing right in front of her.

And somewhere deep inside, she felt that her journey beyond the dusty roads was only just beginning.
