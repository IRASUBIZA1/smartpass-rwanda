Chapter 2: A Love That Must Stay Secret

Every afternoon after school, Amina and David returned to the old mango tree. What started as simple conversations became a friendship they both valued deeply.

They shared their dreams, their fears, and the challenges they faced at home.

"Sometimes I feel like people already decided who I should become," Amina said.

David looked at her and smiled.

"Maybe the world does that, but we have the power to choose our own path."

Their words gave each other strength.

But one day, David noticed something strange.

When he mentioned his family name, Amina became silent.

"Why did you become quiet?" David asked.

Amina looked away.

"There is something you should know."

She explained that her family had a disagreement with another family many years ago.

David's expression changed.

"What family?"

Amina slowly said his family name.

For a moment, they both said nothing.

The person they trusted most came from the family they were told to avoid.

"But we are not our families' mistakes," Amina said.

David nodded.

"I don't want an old story to decide our future."

They promised to keep their friendship safe until they understood the truth.

However, secrets are difficult to hide forever.

Soon, people in the village began noticing that Amina and David spent time together.

Rumours started spreading.

And when their families heard the news, an old conflict that everyone thought was forgotten began to return.

The two young hearts had found hope...

But they were about to discover how difficult it was to protect it.
