📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 6: The Price of a Mistake

For a few seconds, silence filled the air.

Linka stared at the broken camera lying on the ground.

Only then did she realize what she had done.

The camera looked expensive, far beyond anything she could ever afford.

Adrian looked at the damaged device with shock and anger.

No one had ever treated him like that before.

Before either of them could say anything else, Adrian's guards rushed forward.

They had seen the incident from a distance.

"Sir, are you okay?" one of them asked.

Adrian nodded slowly.

The guards immediately turned their attention to Linka.

Within minutes, she was taken to the local police station.

The villagers watched as she was led away.

Some felt sorry for her, while others whispered that she had made a terrible mistake.

At the station, Linka sat quietly, feeling scared and alone.

She had never imagined that a moment of anger could lead her there.

Back at home, her aunt showed little concern.

Instead of worrying about Linka, she complained about the work that would now be left undone.

Two days later, a decision was finally made.

Linka would be released.

However, there was one condition.

She had to pay for the camera she had broken.

And she had only three days to do it.

When she heard the amount, her heart sank.

Even if she worked for years, she could never earn enough money to replace such a camera.

As she walked home, fear filled her mind.

Three days.

That was all she had.

And she had no idea what to do.
