🌄 The Boy Who Discovered The Hidden Valley

A journey of mystery, friendship, courage and discovery.

About The Novel

Ethan was an ordinary student from a small village who loved discovering forgotten stories. While exploring an old school library, he found a mysterious map hidden inside an ancient book.

The map showed the location of a forgotten place known as The Hidden Valley of Amani.

Together with his friend Maya, Ethan begins a dangerous journey filled with secrets, challenges and discoveries that will change his life forever.

Genre: Adventure • Mystery • Friendship • Education
