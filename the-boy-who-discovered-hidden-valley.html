🌄 The Boy Who Discovered The Hidden Valley

A journey of mystery, courage, friendship and discovery.

About The Novel

Ethan is a young student from a small village who loves discovering forgotten stories. One day, he finds an ancient map hidden inside an old school book.

The map leads him and his friend Maya to a mysterious place known as The Hidden Valley of Amani.

Inside the valley, they discover ancient knowledge, face dangerous challenges, and learn that true greatness comes from wisdom, kindness and courage.

Genre: Adventure • Mystery • Friendship • Inspiration • Education
