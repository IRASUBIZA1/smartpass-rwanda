📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 10: An Unexpected Friendship

After leaving the clinic, Linka expected everything to return to normal.

Instead, something unexpected happened.

Adrian began visiting her regularly.

At first, Linka was suspicious.

She could not understand why a wealthy boy from the city would want to spend time with someone like her.

But Adrian kept coming.

They talked about life, dreams, school, and the future.

For the first time in years, Linka felt that someone was truly listening to her.

One afternoon, Adrian noticed her working with pieces of old cloth.

Within minutes, she transformed them into something beautiful.

He watched in amazement.

"You made that yourself?" he asked.

Linka nodded shyly.

"It is just a hobby," she replied.

But Adrian disagreed.

"This is talent," he said.

Over the following days, he discovered more of her abilities.

She could repair clothes, create designs, and turn ordinary materials into impressive creations.

The more time he spent with her, the more he admired her determination.

Meanwhile, Linka began to see a different side of Adrian.

Behind his wealth and confidence was someone who genuinely cared about people.

One morning, Adrian surprised everyone by helping Linka fetch water.

The villagers could hardly believe their eyes.

A rich city boy carrying water containers along dusty village roads was something they had never seen before.

Days later, he even joined her at the local market.

The experience made him appreciate the struggles ordinary people faced every day.

As their friendship grew stronger, Adrian noticed something changing inside him.

Whenever Linka smiled, he felt happy.

Whenever she was sad, he felt concerned.

He did not fully understand these feelings.

But deep inside, his heart was beginning to tell him something he was not yet ready to admit.
