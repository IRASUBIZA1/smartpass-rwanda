📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 9: The Story She Never Told

The clinic room was quiet.

Morning sunlight entered through the window as Linka slowly regained her strength.

Adrian sat nearby, unsure of what to say.

For the first time since they met, there was no anger between them.

Only silence.

After several minutes, Adrian finally spoke.

"Why were you so weak?" he asked gently.

Linka looked away.

For years, she had carried her pain alone.

She had never told anyone the full story.

But something about that moment felt different.

Slowly, she began to speak.

She told him about her mother and how much she loved her.

She described the day illness took her away and how her world changed forever.

She explained how she moved to her aunt's house hoping to find comfort, only to discover a life filled with endless work and harsh treatment.

She spoke about waking before sunrise every day.

About carrying heavy water containers.

About cooking, cleaning, and looking after her aunt's child.

Then she spoke about school.

Tears filled her eyes.

"It was the only place where I felt I had a future," she whispered.

She explained how she had earned a scholarship but was still forced to abandon her education because of the pressure at home.

Adrian listened carefully.

The more he heard, the more ashamed he felt.

He had judged Linka without knowing anything about her struggles.

When she finished speaking, neither of them said a word for a moment.

Finally, Adrian broke the silence.

"You are stronger than anyone I have ever met."

Linka looked surprised.

Nobody had ever said those words to her before.

For years she had only heard criticism and commands.

That simple sentence touched her heart.

Neither of them realized it yet, but a friendship was beginning to grow.

A friendship that would soon change both of their lives.
