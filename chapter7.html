📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 7: The Girl at the Gate

After returning home, Linka spent the entire night thinking about the impossible debt hanging over her head.

Three days was not enough time to earn the money needed to replace Adrian's camera.

Her aunt showed no sympathy.

"If you created the problem, solve it yourself," she said before walking away.

The next morning, Linka made a difficult decision.

She would go to Adrian's house and ask for forgiveness.

The journey felt longer than usual.

When she finally reached the large gate, she could see the beautiful gardens and expensive cars inside.

Everything about the place reminded her how different their worlds were.

She spoke to one of the guards.

"Please tell Adrian that I am here to apologize," she said softly.

The guard disappeared and returned a few minutes later.

"He says there is no forgiveness for what you did. You should leave."

The words hit Linka like a stone.

But she refused to move.

She remained outside the gate under the hot sun.

Minutes turned into hours.

Inside the house, Adrian became irritated when he learned that she was still waiting.

"Why is she still there?" he asked angrily.

Finally, he decided to go outside himself.

As he walked toward the gate, he was determined to make her leave.

He had no idea that the next few moments would change everything.
