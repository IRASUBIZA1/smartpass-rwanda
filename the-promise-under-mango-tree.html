🌹 The Promise Under The Mango Tree

A story of love, courage, and forgiveness.

About The Story

The Promise Under The Mango Tree follows Amina and David, two young people from families separated by an old conflict.

When they meet under an ancient mango tree, they discover that their dreams and hopes are connected. But their love faces a difficult challenge when they learn about the history between their families.

Through courage, honesty, and forgiveness, they must find a way to bring peace between two families.

Genre: Romance • Drama • Friendship • Life Lessons
