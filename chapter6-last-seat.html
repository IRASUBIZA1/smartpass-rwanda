📖 The Last Seat in Class

Chapter 6: Friends From Different Worlds

After winning the academic competition, Samuel's life at Green Valley Secondary School began to change.

Students who once ignored him started asking questions and talking with him.

They discovered that Samuel was not only intelligent but also kind and humble.

However, the biggest surprise came from Kevin.

For a long time, Kevin had believed that success depended on money, popularity, and appearance.

But Samuel showed him something different.

One afternoon after class, Kevin approached Samuel.

"Can I ask you something?" Kevin said.

Samuel looked up from his book.

"Of course."

"How do you stay focused when people judge you?"

Samuel thought for a moment.

"Because I know who I am. Other people's opinions cannot decide my future."

Kevin became quiet.

Those words stayed in his mind.

From that day, the two students started spending more time together.

At first, many students were surprised.

Kevin was one of the richest students in school, while Samuel came from a humble family.

They seemed completely different.

But they discovered that friendship was not built on money.

Samuel helped Kevin understand difficult subjects, while Kevin shared learning materials that Samuel could not afford.

They learned from each other.

One day, Kevin visited Samuel's village to work on a school community project.

For the first time, Kevin saw how hard Samuel's family worked.

He saw Samuel's parents waking up early, farming, and doing everything possible to support their son.

Kevin felt ashamed.

He realized that having more money did not mean someone worked harder or deserved more respect.

That evening, Kevin said something he had never said before.

"Samuel, I think I judged you without knowing your story."

Samuel smiled.

"Many people do that. But we can always choose to understand each other."

From that moment, their friendship became stronger.

Two students from completely different worlds discovered that they had something important in common:

The desire to become better people.

But as their friendship grew, a new challenge was waiting for them.
