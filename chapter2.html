📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 2: A Dream Interrupted

Months passed, and life at her aunt's house became more difficult.

Every morning, Linka woke up before sunrise. She cleaned the house, fetched water, prepared food, and looked after her aunt's child.

The work was exhausting, but what hurt her most was seeing other students walking to school while she remained at home.

One evening, Linka gathered the courage to speak to her aunt.

"Aunt, I want to continue my studies," she said quietly.

Her aunt looked at her with annoyance.

"Who will do the work here?" she replied. "I don't have money for your school fees."

Linka tried to explain how important education was to her, but her aunt refused to listen.

Days later, she was forced to leave school completely.

For the first time, Linka felt that her dreams were slipping away.

That night, she sat alone outside the house and looked at the stars.

Tears filled her eyes as she remembered her mother's words:

"Never stop believing in yourself, Linka. One day your talent will open doors that nobody can close."

Those words gave her strength.

Although she could no longer attend school, she promised herself that she would never stop learning.

She did not know it yet, but destiny was preparing an unexpected opportunity.
