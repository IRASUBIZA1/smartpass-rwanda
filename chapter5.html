📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 5: The Boy From The City

For a few weeks, Linka enjoyed being back at school. She studied hard and quickly became one of the best students in her class.

But life at home became worse.

One afternoon, after returning from school, she found her aunt standing at the door with an angry expression.

Before Linka could say a word, her aunt started shouting.

"You think school is more important than this house?" she yelled.

She complained about the dirty floor, the empty water containers, and the food that had not been prepared.

Then she gave Linka a warning she would never forget.

"The day you choose school over this home will be the day you leave this house forever. If you go back to school, do not come back."

Those words broke Linka's heart.

She spent the night crying, wondering why someone who was supposed to care for her treated her this way.

Days later, unable to endure the pressure, Linka stopped attending school once again.

Meanwhile, a young man named Adrian arrived in the village for a holiday.

He was the son of a wealthy and influential leader and had come from the city to relax away from his busy life.

As he explored the village, he noticed a girl sitting beneath a tree, creating beautiful designs from simple materials.

He was impressed by her talent.

Without thinking much about it, he took a photograph.

The girl immediately stood up and walked toward him.

"What are you doing?" she demanded.

Adrian was surprised by her reaction.

Linka, already frustrated by everything happening in her life, believed he was mocking her.

In a moment of anger, she grabbed the camera.

The struggle lasted only a few seconds.

Then the camera fell to the ground.

A loud crack echoed through the air.

The camera was broken.

Neither of them knew that this single moment would completely change their lives.
