📖 Beyond The Dusty Roads

Chapter 3: The Hidden Talent

Although Linka could no longer attend school, she refused to let her dreams die.

Whenever she finished her chores, she spent time creating things from old papers, pieces of cloth, and materials that other people threw away.

She loved designing clothes and making small models. What looked like waste to others became something beautiful in her hands.

The children in the village were always amazed by her creativity.

"How did you make that?" they often asked.

Linka would simply smile and continue working.

Designing became her escape from the hardships of daily life.

While her aunt saw her only as a helper, Linka secretly imagined herself becoming a famous designer one day.

One afternoon, she created a beautiful design from an old piece of fabric.

As she looked at her work, she felt something she had not felt for a long time — hope.

Maybe her future was not completely lost.

Maybe there was still a path waiting for her beyond the dusty roads of the village.

Little did she know that someone important was about to enter her life and discover the talent that nobody else had noticed.
