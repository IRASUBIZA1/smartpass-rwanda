📖 The Last Seat in Class

A story of dreams, education, and determination.

About The Novel

The Last Seat in Class follows Samuel, a young student from a humble background who joins a new school. Many students judge him because of his appearance and where he sits, but they soon discover that behind the quiet boy is a brilliant mind and a powerful dream.

Genre: Inspirational • Student Life • Drama • Education
